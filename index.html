Dr. Fuad Adelain

Fuad Adelain was born in 1970. He was born in Ethiopia rural area. There was not a school around the village he lived. so he had to travel 8 kilometers on foot to go to school. he was remarkable in school. After finishing his Elementary and high school in the village, he moved to Addis Ababa to attend preparatory. He scored a good mark on the university entrance exam and got a chance to learn in France. He studied in France for 6 years and return to his home country. He worked on different projects in Ethiopia and managed to succeed in all of them.
